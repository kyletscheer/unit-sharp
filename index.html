About Unit Sharp

A mental math trainer for unit conversions. Each round gives you a value to convert — type your best estimate using the calculator keypad and press = to submit.

No calculators, no looking up formulas (well, the 𝑓 button is there if you need a hint). The goal is to build intuition for real-world unit conversions.

HOW SCORING WORKS

Each answer is graded into one of four tiers based on your % error. The tier earns a fixed score, and your session average is the mean of those scores — so 75% means you were consistently hitting Close.

Exact · 100Spot on
Close · 75Within the margin
Ballpark · 40Right idea, off on precision
Off · 0Review the formula

The margin for each tier varies by category — conversions that demand precision (data, angles) have tighter windows than rough real-world ones (nautical, power).

CategoryExactCloseBallpark
Data, Angle≤1%≤5%≤15%
Length, Mass, Volume, Speed, Area≤3%≤10%≤25%
Temp, Time, Nautical, Power≤5%≤18%≤40%

MODES

📚 Practice — Unlimited questions at your own pace. Best for learning new conversions.

⏱ Timed — Answer as many as you can before time runs out. Choose 10–300 seconds.

TIPS

Select specific conversion pairs to drill exactly what you need. Tap a category to expand it, then check individual conversions. Selected pairs appear as pills you can remove anytime.

During a game, tap 𝑓 to reveal the formula as a hint. Tap ‹ Menu or END to exit early.
